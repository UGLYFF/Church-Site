Welcome to RCCG Teens!

At RCCG Teens, we’re more than just a church community—we're a family. We believe in empowering the next generation of leaders, worshippers, and changemakers through the love of Christ. Our mission is to help every teen grow in their faith, discover their God-given talents, and build lifelong relationships rooted in love and purpose. We invite you to join us on this incredible journey of spiritual growth, friendship, and service to God and humanity. Whether it's through our dynamic worship sessions, life-changing events, or vibrant community outreach programs, there’s always something exciting happening here at RCCG Teens.

What We Offer:

Inspiring Worship & Fellowship:

Every week, we come together to worship God and deepen our relationship with Him. It’s a time filled with uplifting music, powerful teachings, and the Holy Spirit’s presence.

Empowering Events:

From youth leadership seminars to prayer vigils and fun hangouts, our events are designed to help you grow in every area of your life—spiritually, socially, and mentally.

Meet Our Team:

Our dedicated team of leaders (Escos) is always here to guide, mentor, and pray with you. Check out our Meet the Escos page to get to know the awesome people making it all happen.

Join Us Today!

If you’re looking for a place to belong, grow, and make a difference, RCCG Teens is the place for you. Follow us on our social media platforms and stay connected to all the latest updates and events. We can’t wait to see you at our next gathering!
